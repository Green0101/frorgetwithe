Got Questions? We've Got Answers.

How long until I see results? +

Most customers see noticeable changes within 4-6 weeks. Full results typically appear after 60-90 days of consistent daily use. Individual results may vary depending on age, hair condition, and the percentage of grey hair.

Is it safe for daily use? +

Absolutely. Forget White is made from 100% natural ingredients with no ammonia, peroxide, or harsh chemicals. It's been dermatologically tested and is suitable for all hair types and sensitive scalps.

Do you deliver across the UK? +

Yes. We deliver to all UK addresses including Northern Ireland, Scotland, and Wales. Standard delivery takes 2-4 working days, and orders over £50 ship free. Express next-day delivery is also available at checkout.

What if it doesn't work for me? +

We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee. If you're not completely satisfied, simply return the product within 30 days for a full refund — no questions asked.

What's the difference between Original and Forte? +

The original Phytokeratine formula is perfect for early-stage greying or sensitive users. Forte is a concentrated formula designed for more advanced grey coverage and faster results. The Miracle Set combines both for maximum effectiveness.
