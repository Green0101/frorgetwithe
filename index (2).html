The Formulation

Why 70% changes
everything.

The hair fibre is composed primarily of keratin — a structural protein that gives each strand its strength, elasticity and shine. When exposed to heat, chemical processes or environmental stress, this protein structure degrades at the cortex level.

Most commercial products contain less than 5% active protein, suspended in water and silicone. Forget White uses a 70% concentrated Phyto-Keratin complex — bio-identical proteins derived from plant sources that are structurally identical to human hair keratin.

The result is genuine cortex repair: not a coating, not a film, but actual structural integration that restores the hair fibre from within.
